Our Mission

At Zypp Electric, our mission is to make every delivery green by replacing petrol scooters with 100% electric scooters on rent. We are solving India’s last-mile delivery challenge with affordable EV rentals, reducing pollution, and building a sustainable future.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every last-mile delivery is powered by clean, electric mobility. Starting in India, Zypp Electric is expanding its EV rental platform globally, making sustainability simple, scalable, and impactful.
