The Opportunity

Three Tailwinds.
One Platform.

India's gig economy is at an inflection point. Three forces are converging — and Zypp is sitting at the exact intersection of all three.

🛵
Quick Commerce Explosion
Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart are growing at 35–40% annually. Every order needs a delivery partner. Every partner needs a reliable vehicle. Zypp is the supply infrastructure that makes 10-minute delivery possible.
⛽
Fuel Price Surge → EV Demand
Petrol has crossed ₹105/litre in metro cities. A petrol bike costs ₹6,000–8,000/month in fuel alone. A Zypp EV cuts that to near-zero. Delivery partners are actively refreshing our app, waiting for Zypp bikes to become available. Demand is outpacing our supply.
📱
The New Middle Class Is Gig
A Zypp rider earning ₹35–45K/month is not a labourer. He is a micro-entrepreneur — building wealth on hustle, not heritage. With Zypp Credit, Zypp CIBIL score, and Zypp Academy, he is entering the formal economy for the first time in his life.

What's Next

The Path to
PAT & IPO.

Four concrete levers. Conservative projections. Measurable monthly milestones.

Now → Sep-26
Fleet Mix Shift to Gen3
Our Gen3 swapping fleet is already PAT positive at ₹+1.09 Cr/month. Retiring older Gen1 vehicles and replacing with Gen3 will mechanically improve company-level PAT — no new cities, no new capital needed.
Jul–Aug 26
AI-Driven Cost Optimisation
The B2C model requires fewer operations staff per vehicle. AI fleet allocation, auto-reconciliation, and smarter routing will reduce our overhead costs significantly while the fleet grows.
Aug–Oct 26
5,000 EVs/Month Deployment
Fuel prices have created a demand wave we have never seen. We are ready to scale from 2,000 EVs/month to 5,000 EVs/month — a 2.5× acceleration backed by real rider demand on the ground.
Sep–Nov 26
Franchise Expansion — Asset Light
Franchise partners fund local fleet and hubs. Zypp earns platform and software fees at near-90% margins. 5 franchise cities launching, expanding to 15+ pipeline cities by FY27. Zero capex from Zypp.
FY28
IPO — India's Gig Economy Story
IPO bankers appointed (Axis Capital + SBI Capital Markets). FY28 listing window. The story that will go public is not "India's largest EV network" — it is "India's first gig economy operating system."
